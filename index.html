Frequently asked.

Will it show under clothing?+

sollio sits close to the body under leggings, dresses, and denim. The fabric is soft and matches most skin tones and undergarment colors.

Is it safe to wear all day?+

sollio offers three heat levels (45–65°C) so you can choose what's comfortable for you. As with any heat product, we recommend the lower settings for extended wear and avoiding sleep on the highest level.

How do I clean it?+

Wipe the fabric shell with a damp cloth after wear. Keep the control pod and adapter dry — they're electronic and shouldn't be submerged.

How does it power on?+

sollio plugs directly into a wall outlet with the included 24V adapter — no batteries or charging to keep track of, so it's ready whenever you are.
